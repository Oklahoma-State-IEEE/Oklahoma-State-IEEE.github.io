Become a Member

We are super excited you are considering joining our IEEE Student Branch here at OSU! Membership has lot's of great perks including access to our lounge, fun workshops, and more!

Perks of Membership

  • Access to IEEE Lounge in GAB 002
  • Added to the IEEE Resume Book
  • Social Events
  • Ability to earn Electrons
  • Able to run for officer positions

How to Join

There are 2 different options when joining IEEE here at OSU. If you join the national IEEE organization, dues are $10. Otherwise, they are $20.

National IEEE Membership Fees: $32 per year, $16 per semester

To join the national branch of IEEE, go to ieee.org and sign up for a student membership. Note, you must obtain your national ID # as proof of membership and list it on your application form.

OSU Membership Fees: $20 or $10 per year

To join the OSU IEEE Student Chapter, fill out this application form.

Paying Fees

National IEEE Membership fees are payed through their website on sign up. OSU Membership Fees can be paid in cash at the beginning of each semester to an officer.
